Oriyan DJ

Crafting Sonic Experiences

Born from an undeniable passion for rhythm and sound, Oriyan DJ started experimenting with beats in small underground clubs. Over the past decade, that raw passion has evolved into a masterclass of electronic mixing, taking stages across the country.

Specializing in House, Techno, and Trance, every set is carefully curated to take the audience on an emotional and energetic journey. It's not just about playing music; it's about controlling the energy of the room.
